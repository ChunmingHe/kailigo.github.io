About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Fu. My current research interests focus on multi-view learning/clustering theory and applications (mainly person re-identification). Prior to this, I obtained my bechelor degree (2010) and master degree (2012) both in Wuhan University, where I worked on image matching and 3D reconstruction, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Yao.
