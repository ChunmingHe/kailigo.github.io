About Me (CV)

I am a master student of the second year in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering at Wuhan University supervised by Prof. Jian Yao. My research interests lie in the areas of computer vision. In particular, my current interests include image feature extraction, matching and reconstruction. Prior to this, I focused my research on object detection.
